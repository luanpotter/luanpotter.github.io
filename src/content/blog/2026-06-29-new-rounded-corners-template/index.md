---
title: New Rounded Corners Template
subtitle: The Cricut pipeline can make rounded corners out of the box
---

While the **Kadomaru Pro** corner cutter is possible one of the most satisfying stationary tools to action, turns out there is a much simpler solution for getting perfectly rounded corners of the exact MTG radius; I speculated about using the Cricut itself to cut rounded corners on previous posts, but never got the time to investigate deeply.

Luckily, Gabriel Grant came to my rescue and made a version of the [MTG 2x3 Cricut template](https://design.cricut.com/landing/project-detail/6a3adb38550b0579d0e35dd5) with the rounded corners built in! That is fully compatible with the template on the card-press side and current workflow, just instructs the Cricut to cut round corners over the same PDF.

I've added as an addendum to the [tutorial](https://luan.xyz/articles/print-and-play-with-cricut) mentioning this new development.
