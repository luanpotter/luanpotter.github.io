Fading

The Fading is a Keyword equivalent to the following effect:

When [this] zone is targeted for a Strike: you can choose not to block.

Rulings

  • Only when the zone [this] card is in is attacked. This effect is not relevant to Active Blocking.
  • If you choose to fade; the _Strike_ becomes a direct attack and cannot be blocked by any other creature.
