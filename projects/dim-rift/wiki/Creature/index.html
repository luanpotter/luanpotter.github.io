Creature

Creatures are one of the three possible Card Types.

Creatures are the most brittle of cards as they can be easily targeted by the opponent, and can also be attacked and destroyed by accruing damage. However, they are the most useful cards as the only ones that can Strike and reduce the opponent's HP, and thus a core component of any deck.

Unlike Artifacts, Creature cards played faced-down cannot be flipped directly (unless they have Ambush). As with any cards, they are automatically flipped at any point if they are targeted (be it by an attack or effect; be by you or your opponent).
