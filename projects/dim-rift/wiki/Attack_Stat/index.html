Attack Stat

The Attack Stat determines how much damage a creature deals during combat.

The damage can either be dealt in the form of damage counters to another creature (in case of a blocked attack), or as a damage to the life points of the player that owns the attacked zone (for unblocked attacks).

For more details on which situations damage is dealt based on the Attack Stat, see Attack Action.

Note that damage dealt to creatures is reduced by their Armor Stat; and compared against their Life Stat.
