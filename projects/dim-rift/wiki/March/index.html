March

March is an Action defined as:

Target an empty, conquered creature zone you control: move [this] to it, and then, optionally, rotate it to a different orientation.

The destination zone must be targeted beforehand, but the final rotation can be chosen at resolution.

A card can "jump over" other cards like a chess Knight to move; but it cannot move to an occupied zone.

Creatures with Explorer can target any (conquered or non-conquered) zones to move regardless of their arrows.
