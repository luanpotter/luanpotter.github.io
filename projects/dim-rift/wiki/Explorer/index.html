Explorer

The Explorer is a Keyword equivalent to the following effect:
[this] can move to inactive zones you control.

Rulings

  • It still must be summoned or set to an active zone first.
  • The zone is still considered inactive.
  • The card can be targeted by effects or attacks regardless of the activation status of its zone.
  • An Explorer card in an inactive zone can later move again and switch places with another card without Explorer, leaving the later in an inactive zone.
