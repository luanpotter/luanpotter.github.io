Explorer

The Explorer is a Keyword equivalent to the following effect:

[this]can target any creature zone you control for its _March_action (including inactive zones).

Inline description: can target any zone to [March]

Rulings

  • It still must be summoned or set to an active zone.
  • An inactive zone with this card is still considered inactive.
  • [this]can be targeted by effects or actions regardless of the activation status of its zone.
