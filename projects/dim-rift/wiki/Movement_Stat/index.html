Movement Stat

The Movement Stat of a card represents how many adjacent, consecutive zones in their respective horizontal row a card can move when taking the Move Action.

Typically only creatures or Autonomous artifacts can perform the Move Action, though that is not a hard rule. Any card with non-transferred Movement Stat and access to the Move Action can perform a movement.
