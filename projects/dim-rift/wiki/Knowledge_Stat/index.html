Knowledge Stat

The Knowledge Stat of a card has two purposes:

  • when the card is summoned, mana equivalent to the value of their Knowledge is collected on the card
  • if the card is set, before it is summoned, you can sac it face-down through the Hidden Knowledge process for paying for costs
