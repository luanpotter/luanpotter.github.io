Draw Phase

The Draw Phase is the last part of a turn, in accordance to the Turn Structure.

This phase serves two purposes:

  • the turn player must draw a card from their deck; decking out does not cause a game loss.
  • effects that trigger "at the end of the turn" or other Fast Speed effects are resolved here.

After the Draw Phase, the turn ends and the next player's turn begins, starting with their Begin Phase.
