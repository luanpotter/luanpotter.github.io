Life Stat

The Life Stat determines how many damage counters a creature can sustain before being destroyed. It is essentially the value of the creature maximum life.

It is important to note that creatures do not have a "current life". The have a number of damage counters and the maximum life. If a creature receives a "+1 Life" bonus, the number of damage counters it has does not change (which means that their effective "current life" does change).
