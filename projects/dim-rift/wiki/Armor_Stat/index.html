Armor Stat

The Armor Stat of a creature causes a damage reduction every time a source deals damage to it.

The damage received by a creature (each time it receives damage) is always reduced by the value of their Armor Stat, down to a minimum of 1 (meaning that every non-zero damage source will cause at least 1 damage to the creature, regardless of the Armor amount).
