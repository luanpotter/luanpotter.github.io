Faction

Factions are Dimensional Rift's version of colors/archetypes. While theoretically you do not need to respect factions at all, and you can just shuffle all cards in the game and draft a completely random deck, the suggested way to play is to use the preconstructed started decks (for now).

The factions have common themes and ideas across their cards but any card should work on any deck. There are no color restrictions.

Cards that do not belong to any of the four factions are considered Unaligned.
