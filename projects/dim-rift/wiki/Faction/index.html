Faction

Factions are Dimensional Rift's version of colors/archetypes. While theoretically you do not need to respect factions at all, and you can just shuffle all cards in the game and draft a completely random deck, the suggested way to play is to have half your deck be predetermined by your choice of faction.

The factions have common themes and ideas across their cards but any card should work on any deck. There are no color restrictions.

The existing factions are:

  • Men
  • Fey
  • Telur
  • Wynn

Cards that do not belong to any of the four factions are considered Unaligned.
