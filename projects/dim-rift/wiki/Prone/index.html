Prone

The Prone is a Condition with the following meaning:
Prone creatures cannot perform actions. They can use an "get up" action to lose their Prone condition.

Rulings

  • Prone creatures are not required to get up, but will remain Prone until they do so.
  • A creature can take the "get up" Action no matter which Event was picked.
  • If a creature is [Inspired], they can perform their second action after "getting up".
