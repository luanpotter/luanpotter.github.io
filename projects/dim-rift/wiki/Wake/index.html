Wake

Wake is an Action defined as:
Flip target creature; it's controller gain 1 life.

Rulings

  • Target creature must be face-down in order to be flipped.
  • The controller gains 1 life only if the creature is flipped.
  • The controller gains 1 life before any When flipped/When summoned effects but after Shadowwalker.
