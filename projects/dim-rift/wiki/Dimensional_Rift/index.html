Dimensional Rift

Dimensional Rift is a 2-player competitive card game in which two duelists challenge each other in a battle of wits, strategy and luck.

It is also a mechanic in the game, associated with the Rift Summoning Event. Lore-wise, it represents the opening of a portal to another dimension that allows certain entities to cross over to our side.

These Rifts can be intra-dimensional (for summoning Allies) or Dimensional Rifts. They can happen intentionally or not and are associated with Soulrifting.
