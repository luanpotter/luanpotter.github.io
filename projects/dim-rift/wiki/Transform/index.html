Transform

The Transform is a Keyword equivalent to the following effect:

When the transform condition is met, you must immediately resolve: "Sac [this]: you can summon from your Fables Deck a Transformed creature."

Rulings

  • As with any triggers, this happens at Fast Speed, regardless of phase or turn.
  • This does not count as your one Fabled summon during Deployment.
