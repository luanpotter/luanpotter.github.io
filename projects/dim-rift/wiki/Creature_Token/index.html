Creature Token

Creature Tokens are creatures on the battlefield that are not represented by real cards that participate in the Card Lifecycle. They are often represented by token cards, which are considered game pieces that only exist while on the Battlefield and can never go into the decks, graveyard, or hands.

Creature Tokens can only exist while on the battlefield and cease to exist when they leave the battlefield (i.e. when they are destroyed). That means they are not put in the graveyard but back into the supply pile where they came from. They are not considered "cards" or part of any "deck", but rather game pieces, like markers and counters. However, for practical purposes, it is recommended to keep your tokens in your Fables Deck for easy access.

Creature Tokens are always 1 Might / 1 Life creatures with no effect and one single top arrow, regardless of how they are created or represented.

Creature Tokens are always summoned face-up in any orientation, and therefore can never be Face-Down or flipped.
