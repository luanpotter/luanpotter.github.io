Draw

Draw means moving the top card of your (main) deck pile into your hand secretly (i.e. not revealing it to your opponent). It is a game action part of the Card Lifecycle.

Drawing cards is limited to exactly three circumstances:

  • End of Turn: At the end of every turn, during the Draw Phase, the turn player draws 1 card.
  • Campaign with Full Conquest: When taking the Campaign Event with all 6 conquerable zones already conquered, the player draws 1 card instead of conquering.
  • Taking a Hit: When a player takes a direct hit (loses 1 HP from a Strike), they draw 1 card.

Dimensional Rift does not include any draw, filtering, or stacking effects on cards. No card effect can cause a player to draw additional cards, look, or manipulate deck order.
