Draw

Draw means moving the top card of your (main) deck pile into your hand secretly (do not reveal to your opponent). It is a game action part of the Card Lifecycle.

Drawing can be done by 3 different means:

  • at the start of a player's turn (except for Preparations): that player draw 1 card
  • by taking the Campaign Event, if a player has 11 Summoning Tokens: that player draws 1 card instead of gaining another token
  • due to card effects that contain the word "Draw"
