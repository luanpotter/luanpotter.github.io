Maneuver

Maneuver is an Action defined as:
Move target artifact as if it had Movement equal to at least this card's Movement.
Maneuver is commonly associated with the Telur faction.

Rulings

  • This is considered a Move Action being performed by the artifact (but does not consume their turn's Action).
  • If the artifact already has more Movement than [this], you can use the greater number.
  • [this] does not need to connect to the destination zone.
  • You can switch with another artifact.
