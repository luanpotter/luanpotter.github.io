Blessed

The Blessed is a Condition with the following meaning:
A creature is "Blessed" if and only if another creature you control with at least 1 mana connects to it. The "Blessed" effect is active if and only if the creature is "Blessed".
Blessed is commonly associated with the Men faction.

Rulings

  • Creates are considered to be Blessed if they satisfy the condition if if they do not have a "Blessed" effect.
  • Two creatures you control can "bless" each other if they both have mana and the appropriate arrows.
