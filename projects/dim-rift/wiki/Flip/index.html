Flip

Flip means turning a face-down card on the battlefield face-up, thereby summoning it.

This is the order of operations to follow:

  • reveal the card to both players
  • choose to activate the Shadowwalker effect if any, and resolve that before continuing
  • chose the desired orientation
  • physically flip the card and put it back on its zone on the chosen orientation
  • activate any "When flipped" effects
  • collect Mana Counters on the card if it has any Mana Stat
  • activated any "When summoned" effects

Different card types have different flipping requirements:

  • Monuments can never be face-down, and thus can never be flipped.
  • Both artifacts and creatures must be flipped when they are targeted.
  • Artifacts can be proactively flipped at Fast Speed; in fact, even as part of activating one of its effects.
  • Creatures can only be flipped when targeted (unless they have Ambush).
