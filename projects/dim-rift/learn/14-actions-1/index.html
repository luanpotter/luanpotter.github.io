Actions (1)

During the Event Phase, your cards get to act. Each face-up card you control can perform one action per event. You can declare actions in any order and not every card needs to act.

Action Pool

The Action Pool is the set of actions a card has available. It is combined from two sources:

  1. Printed actions on the card itself (marked with );
  2. Bonus actions granted by your event choice this turn.

Some cards have the March or Strike actions printed on them, which means they can perform those actions every event. Some have other standard or bespoke actions available.

March

All your face-up creatures get the March action to your pool when you choose the Campaign Event.

Target an empty, conquered creature zone you control: move this card to it, selecting any desired orientation. The destination zone must be targeted beforehand, but the final rotation can be chosen at resolution. A card can "jump over" other cards like a chess Knight; but it cannot move to an occupied zone.

Creatures with Explorer can target any (conquered or non-conquered) zones you control to move, regardless of their arrows.
