The Cards

Every card in Dimensional Rift has a type, stats, effects, and arrows.

Types

  • Creatures - the main fighters in your front-row; they can Strike and Block, and sustain Damage.
  • Artifacts - supporting cards in your back-row; they provide effects and benefits;
  • Monuments - a one-per-player card that can affect the entire battlefield.

Stats

  • Might - how much damage this creature deals when fighting
  • Life - how much damage this creature can take before being destroyed
  • Will - how much mana this card collects when summoned or sacrificed (used to pay for costs)

Arrows

Creature and artifact cards have arrows indicating the directions and zones that they can target. Monuments do not have arrows and do not participate in the arrow targeting system.

Effects

Every card has one or more effects. This can include summoning costs, additional actions, continuous buffs, triggered responses, or proactive activations.
