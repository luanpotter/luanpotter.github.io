Actions (1)

Strike

All your face-up creatures get the Strike action to your pool when you choose the Battle Event. To Strike:

  1. You target a creature zone (typically your opponent's)
  2. If the targeted zone has a creature, that creature must block the attack (passive block)
  3. If not, another creature can actively block if they connect to the targeted zone
  4. If there is a blocker, both creatures fight
  5. If there is no blocker, the controller of the targeted zone is hit for 1 HP

Fighting

In a fight, both creatures deal damage equal to their Might Stat to each other simultaneously. If at any point (as a state-based action) the damage counters on a creature equal or exceed their Life Stat, they are destroyed. Note that damage counters are NOT cleared at any step and stay forever.

Other Common Actions

  • Research: Draw a card, then discard a card
  • Equip: Attach an Equipment to target creature you control
  • Conscript: Summon a Creature Token to target empty conquered creature zone you control
  • Pray: Collect 1 mana on this card
