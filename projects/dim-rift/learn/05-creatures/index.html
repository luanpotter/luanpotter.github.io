Creatures

Creatures constitute your front-row, and are the most brittle card type as they can be easily targeted by the opponent, as well as being destroyed by accruing damage. However, they are the most useful cards as the only ones that can Strike and reduce the opponent's HP, and thus a core component of any deck.

  • Identified by the red title bar and the creature icon on the left
  • Can be played Face-Up (Summoned) or Face-Down (Set) to your Conquered creature zones
  • When face-down, a creature cannot be flipped unless it is targeted (by any effect or action by any player)
  • If at any point a creature on the field has damage counters equal or exceeding their Life stat, they are Destroyed immediately as a state-based action and sent to the Graveyard.
