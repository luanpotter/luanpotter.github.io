Related Terms

Manalock: Target card cannot consume its own mana or activate effects that cost mana until the end of turn. If that card has an effect in this chain that would qualify, that effect fizzles.

Autonomous: The Movement stat printed on this card is considered both transferred and not transferred. If [this] is not equipped or crewed: all transferred effects on [this] can be used as if they were not transferred. [this] can perform the Move Action. When destroyed: collect Souls according to rank.

Explorer: This card can move to non-activated zones you control.
