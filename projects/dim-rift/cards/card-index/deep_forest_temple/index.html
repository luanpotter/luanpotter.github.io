Deep Forest Temple

Name: Deep Forest Temple

Type: Monument

Faction: Fey

Deck: Extra Deck

Stats:

Arrows:

    Cost
    Sac or discard 5 cards: summon.
    Continuous
    Prone creatures cannot be sac'ed or consume mana for the purposes of Rift Summoning.
    Continuous
    Prone creatures can Transform at the end of each turn.
    Action
    Choose a creature that has a Connection Chain to a face-up artifact you control: chosen creature is knocked Prone.

    Related Terms

    Prone: Prone creatures cannot perform actions. They can use an "get up" action to lose their Prone condition.

    Transform: When the transform condition is met, you must immediately resolve: "Sac [this]: you can summon from your Extra Deck a [Transformed] creature"
