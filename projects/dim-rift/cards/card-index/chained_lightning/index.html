Chained Lightning

Name: Chained Lightning

Type: Artifact - Scroll

Faction: Unaligned

Deck: Main Deck

Stats: 3

Arrows:

  • top: single
Mandatory Once-per-turn Trigger
When summoned: deal 2 damage to target and it consumes 2 mana. After this effect resolves: You must target a new card from the arrows of the previous target. You cannot re-target the same zone during each chain.
