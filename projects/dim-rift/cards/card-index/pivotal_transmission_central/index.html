Pivotal Transmission Central

Name: Pivotal Transmission Central

Type: Monument

Faction: Telur

Deck: Extra Deck

Stats:

Arrows:

    Cost
    Sac 3 cards, at least one of which with Conduit: summon.
    Action
    For each card on the field, choose either (1) it has or (2) it loses Conduit.
    Action
    Permanently Manalock chosen card with Conduit.
    Continuous
    Cards with Conduit count as two for the purposes of paying Rift Summoning sacrificial costs.

    Related Terms

    Conduit: A card connected to [this] can use this card's arrows for their targeting.

    Manalock: Target card cannot consume its own mana or activate effects that cost mana until the end of turn. If that card has an effect in this chain that would qualify, that effect fizzles.
