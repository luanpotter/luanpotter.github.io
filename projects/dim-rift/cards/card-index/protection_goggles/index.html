Protection Goggles

Name: Protection Goggles

Type: Artifact - Equipment

Faction: Telur

Deck: Main Deck

Stats: 1 1 2

Arrows:

  • top: single
  • topRight: single
  • topLeft: single
  • bottom: double
Mandatory Once-per-turn Trigger
When summoned: Manalock target.
Action
Target a face-down card: you can see target; then: its controller gain 1 life.
Continuous
[equipped] can Operate.
Optional Once-per-turn Trigger
When [this] is destroyed by your opponent: draw a card.

Related Terms

Manalock: Target card cannot consume its own mana or activate effects that cost mana until the end of turn. If that card has an effect in this chain that would qualify, that effect fizzles.

Operate: Select an Activation effect or Action from target artifact: resolve that effect. Ignore any costs or OPT restrictions.
