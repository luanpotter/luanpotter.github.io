Deep Forest Temple

Name: Deep Forest Temple

Type: Monument

Faction: Fey

XD: Extra Deck

Arrows:

    Cost
    Sac a Transformed creature: summon.
    Continuous
    Prone creatures cannot be sac'ed or consume mana for the purposes of XD summoning.
    Continuous
    Prone creatures can Transform at the end of each turn.
    Fast Once-per-turn Activation
    Choose a creature that has a Connection Chain to a face-up artifact you control: chosen creature falls Prone.

    Related Keywords

    Transformed: Can only be summoned using a [Transform] effect.

    Prone: Prone creatures cannot move or rotate during their next Move Action (they must instead spend it to get up). They can still be moved or rotated by effects.

    Transform: When the transform condition is met, you must immediately sac [this] and you can summon from your XD a [Transformed] creature.
