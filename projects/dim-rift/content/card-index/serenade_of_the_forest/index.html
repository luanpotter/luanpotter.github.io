Serenade of the Forest

Name: Serenade of the Forest

Type: Artifact - Scroll

Faction: Fey

XD: Main Deck

3

Arrows:

  • top: double
  • topRight: double
  • topLeft: double
Mandatory Once-per-turn Trigger
When summoned; choose either: (1) target creature currently Prone cannot get up (ever) or (2) target Transform creature is considered Transformed and cannot trigger that ability anymore.

Related Keywords

Prone: Prone creatures cannot move or rotate during their next Move Action (they must instead spend it to get up). They can still be moved or rotated by effects.

Transform: When the transform condition is met, you must immediately sac [this] and you can summon from your XD a [Transformed] creature.

Transformed: Can only be summoned using a [Transform] effect.
