Attack Reaction

Name: Attack Reaction

Type: Artifact - Trap

Faction: Unaligned

XD: Main Deck

Arrows:

  • top: single
  • topRight: double
  • topLeft: double
Mandatory Once-per-turn Trigger
If target creature blocks an attacker: that creature gets either (1) +2 Attack or (2) +2 Armor until end of turn.
Mandatory Once-per-turn Trigger
If target creature declares an Attack Action against a blocker: that creature gets either (1) +2 Attack or (2) +2 Armor until end of turn.
