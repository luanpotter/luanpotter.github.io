Cylindrical Concoction

Name: Cylindrical Concoction

Type: Artifact - Trap

Faction: Unaligned

XD: Main Deck

Arrows:

    Mandatory Once-per-turn Trigger
    If you are dealt damage by your opponent: all damage dealt to you this chain by your opponent is dealt to your opponent instead, and vice-versa (note: this does not include paying costs.).
    Mandatory Once-per-turn Trigger
    If a creature is dealt X damage: you gain X life.
