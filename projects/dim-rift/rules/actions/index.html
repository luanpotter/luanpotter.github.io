Actions

Each face-up card on the field that you control can potentially perform one action per Event. Actions are performed at Slow Speed and can only be performed during the Event Phase, only when the stack is empty (similar to Summoning/Set on the Deployment Phase).

The set of possible actions each card can perform during a given Event is called their Action Pool, and is combined from two sources:

  • the explicit Actions that are printed on the card;
  • the additional action that are granted by choice of Event (either March or Strike).

For example, a creature with "Action: March" could choose that action every Event. But a creature that does not have "Action: March" printed on it only gets to access the March action during a Campaign Event.

Some creatures have special actions that are described on their card, such as Pray or Research. These are in their Action Pool and can be chosen instead of the regular March or Strike actions.

A player does not need to perform an Action with every available card (or any at all). Only face-up cards already on the field can perform actions. Typically, only creatures will have actions (but some artifacts or monuments, specially Equipments will have that option as well).

Other Notable Actions

Research: Draw a card; then, discard a card.

Equip: Attach [this] to target creature.

Conscript: Summon a Creature Token to target empty active creature zone.

Pray: [this] collects 1 mana.

Maneuver: Re-organize any number of connected artifacts you control into any number of connected artifact zones.
