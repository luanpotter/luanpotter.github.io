Some common phrasing and elements appear across the effects, card text and rules. In an attempted to standardize wording, this document contains a list of preferred terms and expressions to be used to convey certain ideas.

Capitalization

  • card types (creature, artifact, monument) should be lowercase

References

  • [this] is used to refer to the card itself. Sometimes it can also be used to refer to "this card's zone"
  • [equipped] is used to refer to the card either equipped or crewed by [this]

Costs & Effects

  • consume [any] [x in numerical form] mana

  • collect [x in numerical form] mana on [...]

  • pay [x in numerical form] life

  • [you/your opponent/player] gain [x in numerical form] life

  • deal [x in numerical form] damage to (e.g. "deal 1 damage to target")

  • note: on very rare cases, non-creature cards will use damage counters. for those cases the wording should be put [x] damage counter[s] on [this].

  • [this/creature] heals [x in numerical form/all] damage (e.g. "[this] heals 2 damage")

  • has [Keyword] (e.g. "Target creature has [Conduit].")

  • can [Action] (e.g. "Target can [Attack].")

  • gets [Condition] (e.g. "Target card gets [Manalock]") -- TODO: review this

  • gets +/- [x in numerical form] [Stat] (e.g. "Target creature gets +1 Attack.")

Common Triggers

  • When summoned: [...]
  • When flipped: [...]
  • When destroyed: [...]
  • After [this] battles with another creature:

Turn Phases

  • until the end of turn
  • At the the end of [your] turn

Combat Triggers

  • After this battles
