Tile Tableau

The Tile Tableau is a display of all available tiles that can be purchased during the Market Phase.

The previous tiers Tableaus are still available after a new tier is unlocked.

Purchasing Rules

  • a tile of tier X costs X+1 coins
  • when you buy a pipe, you receive a random pipe piece
  • the first pipe you buy each round costs 1 less
  • [for 4 or more players] you can only buy one of each machine
