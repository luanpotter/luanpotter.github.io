Chemistry Panel

Chemistry Panels, or simply Panels, are each of the "formulae" that might define a type of potion that the players can brew.

Other than the first 3 Panels, which are set, the rest of them are randomized and unlocked as the game progresses.

For a complete reference of all panels available, check the Panels page.
