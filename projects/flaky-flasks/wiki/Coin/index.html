Coin

Coins are a numerical resource players can possess and use to pay for costs. They are represented in game by coin tokens.

Coins can be spent:

  • to buy tiles from the Tile Tableau
  • to buy cards from the Card River
  • in bargains with other players

Coins are used as a tiebreaker in case of a tie in Fame at the end of the game.

Coins can be gained:

  • by selling potions during the Market Phase
  • in bargains with other players

Note that Coin cards do not award permanent coin tokens but rather temporary coin markers that are cleaned up after the Market Phase.

Players start with zero Coins and cannot go below zero. The amount of coins is always a natural number.
