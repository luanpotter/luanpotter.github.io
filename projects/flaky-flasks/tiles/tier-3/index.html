Tier 3 - Tiles

Combination Centrifuge
A fluid centrifuge; combines two fluids into one (cycle colors if mismatch; sum the potency).

Mana Crucible
A powerful crucible oozing with a magical aura; melt any symbols into raw energy.
Consumes a symbol of value X to generate X / 2 units of power, rounded up.

Thaumaturgical Imbuer
A mystical imbuer. [power-x]: increases the potency of a fluid by X.

Pneumatic Matrix
An inscrutable array of pipes and tubes.
[power]: redistributes input symbol to any direction.
As an alternate cost to power, you can discard a card.

Tesla Coil
A powerful, mysterious coil; redistributes input power to any adjacent tile.

Auric Transmutator
Powered by a shard of the Philosopher's Stone; consumes a connected coin to generate that amount of fame.
[power]: generate that amount of fame + 1 instead; also, unlock all inputs.
