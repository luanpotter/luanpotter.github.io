Tier 1 - Tiles

Alchemical Filter
A simple pipe filter; allows you to choose to void any symbols that pass through.
Per operation, you can consume a Charge: enable locked outputs.

Ingredient Guillotine
A small guillotine for symbols; splits a symbol into two equal halves, rounded down.
For each symbol that comes, two (or zero) symbols come out of same color and shape but value / 2 rounded down.
A zero-valued symbol ceases to exist. Outputs can exit through any output direction.
Per operation, you can consume a Charge: you can round up instead.

Stamping Press
A press that joins two symbols together; takes in two symbols of same color and shape and outputs a symbol of that color and shape with the added values.
Per operation, you can consume a Charge: you can join up to 3 symbols instead.

Mortar & Pestle
A mundane mortar and pestle; grinds down shapes adding rough edges.
Outputs a symbol of same color and value but shape with +1 side.
▲ -> ■ -> ⬟ -> [void]
Must operate. ⬟ are voided.
Per operation, you can consume a Charge: you can choose not to operate.

Sandpaper Belt
A rustic, manually operated sandpaper on a belt; polish shapes by removing one edge.
Outputs a symbol of same color and value as the input but with its shape reduced by one side.
I.e., ⬟ -> ■ -> ▲ -> [void]
Must operate. ▲ are voided.
Per operation, you can consume a Charge: you can choose not to operate.

Tallow Candle
A faint tallow candle.
Each Round, at the Setup Phase: generate 1 Charge.
