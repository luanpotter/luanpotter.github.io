Combination Centrifuge [Tier 3]

A fluid centrifuge; combines two fluids into one (cycle colors if mismatch; sum the potency).
When powered you can choose the output color.

Rulings

  • Must operate with exactly two fluids. Void fluids at end of round.
  • If the colors of both fluids are the same, the output fluid will have the same color (e.g. red + red = red).
  • If the colors of both fluids are different, the output fluid will have the missing color (e.g. red + blue = green).
  • The potency of the output fluid is the sum of the potencies of the input fluids.
